Private Waters

In 1984 The Fly Shop® recognized that the demand for angling privacy and quality fishing had far outstripped all but the most remote public resources. Then we did something about it. With that in mind, we intensified our commitment to protecting existing fisheries, and our promotion of the catch-and-release concept.
